How to Use

  1. Enter the total story points for your sprint
  2. Specify the sprint duration in days
  3. Add daily progress entries with remaining story points
  4. Click Generate Chart to visualize your progress

Understanding the Chart

  • Ideal Line: Shows the expected rate of progress
  • Actual Line: Shows your team's actual progress
  • Above Ideal: Indicates slower than expected progress
  • Below Ideal: Indicates faster than expected progress
