Bradford Factor Thresholds

  • Under 50: Acceptable
  • 50-100: Concerning
  • 100-200: Serious
  • Over 200: Critical

About the Bradford Factor

The Bradford Factor is a human resources metric used to measure employee absenteeism. It places more weight on frequent short-term absences than on long-term absences, as these are often more disruptive to business operations.
