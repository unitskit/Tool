How to Use

  1. Enter your total number of visitors or leads
  2. Enter the number of converted visitors/customers
  3. Input your customer acquisition cost
  4. Enter your average order value
  5. Select the type of conversion you're measuring
  6. Click Calculate to view the analysis

Understanding the Results

  • Conversion Rate: The percentage of visitors who complete the desired action
  • Cost per Conversion: The average cost to acquire one conversion
  • ROI: Return on investment for your conversion efforts
  • Revenue per Visitor: Average revenue generated per visitor
