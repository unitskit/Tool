How to Use

  1. Select your budget period (monthly, quarterly, or annual)
  2. Set your target profit margin
  3. Add your income sources
  4. Add fixed expenses (costs that don't vary with sales)
  5. Add variable expenses (costs that vary with sales)
  6. Click Calculate to analyze your budget

Understanding the Budget

  • Fixed Expenses: Costs that remain constant regardless of sales volume
  • Variable Expenses: Costs that change proportionally with sales
  • Profit Margin: Net profit as a percentage of total revenue
  • Break-even Point: Sales level where revenue equals total expenses
  • Budget Variance: Difference between planned and actual amounts
