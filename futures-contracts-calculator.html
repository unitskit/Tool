Futures Price Formula

Futures Price:

F = S × (1 + r × t) + C - Y

Where:

  • F = Futures Price
  • S = Spot Price
  • r = Risk-Free Rate
  • t = Time to Expiry (in years)
  • C = Storage Cost
  • Y = Convenience Yield
