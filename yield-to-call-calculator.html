Formula Reference
Yield to Call (YTC)

The YTC is the internal rate of return (IRR) that equates:

Market Price = Î£(Coupon Payments) + Call Price

Variables:
  • P = Market price
  • C = Annual coupon payment
  • n = Number of periods to call
  • CP = Call price
  • r = Yield to call (solved for)
