Understanding Burn Rate

  • Gross Burn Rate: Total monthly cash consumption
  • Net Burn Rate: Monthly cash consumption minus revenue
  • Runway: Months until cash reserves are depleted
  • Positive Burn Rate: Indicates cash consumption exceeds revenue
  • Negative Burn Rate: Indicates revenue exceeds cash consumption
